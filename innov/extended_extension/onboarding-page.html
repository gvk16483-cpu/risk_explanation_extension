✉️ Turn on Gmail access

To read and summarize emails, the extension needs permission to access your Gmail tab. You stay in control and can turn this off anytime.

1
Click “Enable on Gmail”.
Chrome will open Gmail if it is not already open.
2
Pin the extension (optional).
Use the puzzle icon in Chrome’s toolbar and pin “Email Reader”.
🔐
This extension only reads the email you have open in mail.google.com.
No data is stored or sent anywhere else without your action.
